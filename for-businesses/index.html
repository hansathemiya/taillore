Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about partnering with Tailors.lk

General Questions

Can I join as a wholesale fabric supplier?
Yes! We have dedicated B2B tools, tender access, and bulk listing options for fabric suppliers, wholesalers, and textile merchants.
Can I upgrade/downgrade my plan anytime?
Yes. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle. You can manage your subscription from your dashboard.
What does the "+" mean on pricing?
Base prices are listed, but final pricing may vary based on location, business size, customization needs, and service scope. Contact us for a precise quote.

Tender Questions

Do I pay commission on tender orders?
No. Tender partners pay an annual license fee and keep 100% of order revenue (minus material costs). Standard platform orders have a 10% facilitation fee.
When do tenders open?
Major tenders open annually around May 20th. Private tenders can be requested anytime through our platform.

Verification Questions

How does verification work for the Verified Badge?
We conduct an unannounced visit to assess craftsmanship, hygiene, customer service, and business legitimacy. Full checklist provided post-application.
Why are there only 10-15 slots?
To maintain high quality standards. We cannot verify more shops without compromising our audit quality. Slots fill quickly each month.

Learn Program Questions

Do I need teaching experience to join Tailors.lk Learn?
No. We provide training and support for skilled tailors who want to teach. We'll help you develop curriculum and teaching skills.
How much can I earn as an instructor?
Earnings vary based on course type, enrollment, and session format. Most instructors earn LKR 15,000-75,000/month part-time, with top instructors earning significantly more.
