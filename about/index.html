About Tailors.lk

Building a digital home for Sri Lanka's tailoring community

Our Mission

Tailors.lk is building a digital home for Sri Lanka's tailoring community. Thousands of tailors work without visibility. No websites. No maps. No reviews. Just word of mouth. We're here to change that.

Our mission is to connect customers with trusted local tailors, providing a platform that celebrates the craftsmanship and expertise of Sri Lankan tailors while making their services easily accessible to everyone.

What We Do

We provide a comprehensive directory of tailors across Sri Lanka, from major cities like Colombo and Kandy to smaller towns and villages. Our platform allows customers to search for specific services, view tailor profiles, and connect directly with professionals.

For tailors, we offer a simple way to create a professional online presence, reach new customers, and grow their business through our verified directory.
