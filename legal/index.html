Legal & Platform Policies

Tailors.lk operates as a digital marketplace connecting customers, tailors, brands and partners across Sri Lanka.

This page explains our legal framework, responsibilities, limitations and platform rules governing the use of Tailors.lk as Sri Lanka's premier tailoring marketplace. Including Tender Guidelines, Partner Terms, and Tailor Black Governance.

Platform Introduction

Tailors.lk is a technology and discovery platform that connects customers with professional tailors, brands, and service providers across Sri Lanka. Our platform operates as a digital marketplace facilitating connections, communication, and service discovery.

We provide digital infrastructure for listings, lead routing, communication tools, and platform features that enhance the tailoring experience. Our role is to facilitate connections while maintaining a secure, reliable platform for all users.

By The Numbers

  • 300+ Verified Partners
  • 9 Cities Across Sri Lanka
  • Rs. 45M+ Revenue Generated for Partners
  • Rs. 15,000-75,000/month Average Instructor Earnings

Important: Tailors.lk does not directly stitch, manufacture, or deliver tailoring services unless explicitly stated in specific partnership arrangements. We are a discovery and connection platform. For Tender & Buyout Campaigns, Tailors.lk acts as the principal facilitator, managing the tender award, contract, and security deposit process as an agent for the partner.

Financial Terms

  • Entry Fee: Minimum LKR 5,000 per tender bid Non-refundable
  • License Bid: Annual license fee (LKR 50,000 to 500,000+ depending on category)
  • Security Deposit: Refundable deposit (10-20x entry fee, e.g., LKR 75,000-100,000)
  • Commission: 0% on tender orders

Revocation Triggers

A tender may be revoked immediately for:

  • Quality Failure: Multiple complaints, substandard materials
  • Delivery Failure: Missing deadlines, >20% volume unable to fulfill
  • Ethical Breach: Fraudulent documentation, unauthorized subcontracting
  • Client Solicitation: Direct contact with tender-sourced clients
  • Non-Cooperation: Failure to respond within 48 hours

Re-award Process

If revoked, tender may be offered to 2nd Highest Bidder (48 hours to accept). Original winner barred from bidding for 12 months.

Private Tenders

Year-round opportunities for niche or urgent orders. Same rules apply: Entry Fee, License Bid, Deposit, Performance Expectations, and Reporting.

Tailor Black Governance Privilege

Exclusive Opposition Right

Only partners holding TAILOR BLACK status possess the privilege to formally oppose a tender award.

Valid Grounds:

  • Proven capacity misrepresentation by winner
  • Documented quality failures on prior platform work
  • Ethical breaches (fraud, harassment, etc.)
  • Conflict of interest

Invalid Grounds:

  • Disagreement with bid amount
  • Personal rivalry or preference
  • Speculation without evidence

Review Process

  1. SUBMISSION: Submit opposition with evidence to black@tailors.lk
  2. PRELIMINARY REVIEW: 3 business days validity check
  3. INVESTIGATION: 7-10 business days if valid
  4. DECISION: Upheld (re-evaluated) or Dismissed
  5. CONFIDENTIALITY: Details kept confidential, no retaliation

Warning: Abuse of this privilege may result in Tailor Black status review. This status cannot be bought—it is earned through consistent excellence and invitation-only review.

Brand Attribution & Client Protection

Mandatory Brand Attribution

All tender documents MUST display:

  • "Fulfilled by [Partner Name] via Tailors.lk"
  • Tailors.lk logo on official documents
  • Platform order reference numbers
  • Customer service routed through platform channels

Client Solicitation Restriction

For contract duration + 12 months after completion, partners agree NOT to:

  • Directly solicit tender-sourced clients for work outside platform
  • Offer "private deals" to bypass platform
  • Build independent client databases from tender orders

Violation Consequences

  • Immediate tender revocation
  • Forfeiture of Security Deposit
  • Account suspension or termination
  • Legal action for breach of contract

Platform Role & Disclaimer

  • Discovery Platform: We connect customers with independent tailors but do not provide tailoring services directly.
  • Service Provider Independence: All services executed by independent tailors.
  • Quality Responsibility: Service quality belongs to the service provider, not Tailors.lk.
  • Tender Facilitation: For Tender & Buyout Campaigns, Tailors.lk acts as principal facilitator managing awards, contracts, and security deposits.
