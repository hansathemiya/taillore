Frequently Asked Questions

✅ Listing & Verification

How long does it take to get listed?
We review submissions manually. You’ll hear back within a few days.
Do I need to upload photos or documents?
Not yet. We’ll contact you if we need anything additional.
Can I list my tailoring business even if I work from home?
Yes—home-based tailors are welcome.
Is there a limit to how many services I can select?
No. Choose all that apply to your business.

✅ Updates & Changes

Can I update my listing later?
Yes. You’ll be able to request edits anytime.
What if I move to a new location?
Just let us know—we’ll update your profile.

✅ Cost & Access

Is it free to join?
Yes. There’s no charge to be listed.
Will I need to pay later?
We’ll always be transparent about any future changes. Right now, it’s free.

✅ Visibility & Reach

Where will my profile appear?
On Tailors.lk’s public directory once it goes live.
Can customers contact me directly?
Yes. We’ll display your preferred contact method.

✅ Support & Contact

Who do I contact if I have questions?
You can reach us via WhatsApp or email anytime.
Can I remove my listing if I change my mind?
Absolutely. Just send us a request.
