  1. BROWSE: Visit tailors.lk/tenders and review active tender cards

  3. SUBMIT PRIVATE BID: Enter confidential license bid amount (must exceed Minimum Bid)

  5. AWAIT RESULTS: Winners notified via email & dashboard by May 21st
  6. SIGN CONTRACT & PAY DEPOSIT: Winners receive formal contract + invoice. Pay Refundable Security Deposit by June 1st
  7. EXECUTE & REPORT: Fulfill orders per timeline and submit quarterly revenue data

7.4 Eligibility Requirements

  • Active Partner Account: Must have active Tailors.lk partner account
  • Business Registration: Valid business registration (BR/NIC) required
  • Capacity Documentation: Proof of production capacity (staff count, machine inventory, workspace)
  • Minimum Rating: 4.0+ average customer rating (if previously active)
  • No Violations: No unresolved complaints or policy violations
  • Revenue Reporting: Agreement to submit quarterly revenue data

7.5 Revocation Triggers

A tender may be revoked immediately, with full or partial forfeiture of Security Deposit, for:

  • Quality Failure: Multiple complaints about craftsmanship, use of substandard materials without disclosure, failure to meet specifications
  • Delivery Failure: Missing deadlines without prior communication, inability to fulfill >20% of assigned volume, abandonment of contract
  • Ethical Breach: Fraudulent documentation, unauthorized subcontracting, harassment, discrimination, or illegal practices
  • Client Solicitation: Direct contact with tender-sourced clients for outside work (removing Tailors.lk branding, offering private deals)
  • Non-Cooperation: Failure to respond to platform mediation within 48 hours, refusal to provide evidence
  • Revenue Reporting Failure: Failure to report for 2+ consecutive quarters

7.6 Re-award Process

If a tender is revoked:

  1. Original winner notified of revocation and deposit forfeiture
  2. Offer extended to 2nd Highest Bidder from original cycle (if bid remains valid)
  3. Second bidder has 48 hours to accept and pay deposit
  4. If declined, tender may be re-listed or cancelled
  5. Original winner barred from bidding on any Tailors.lk tenders for 12 months

7.7 Brand Attribution & Client Protection

All tender-sourced work must adhere to:

  • Mandatory Branding: All contracts, invoices, delivery notes must display "Fulfilled by [Partner Name] via Tailors.lk" with platform logo
  • Platform Order References: Use Tailors.lk-provided order reference numbers
  • Client Solicitation Restriction: For contract duration + 12 months after completion, partners agree NOT to directly solicit, contact, or re-engage any customer acquired through a Tailors.lk tender for work outside the platform
  • Prohibited: Removing Tailors.lk branding, presenting tender work as "direct" partnership, using client lists for external marketing
  • Consequences: Immediate tender revocation, forfeiture of Security Deposit, account suspension/termination, legal action for breach of contract

7.8 Revenue Reporting Requirements

9. Tailor Black Governance Privilege

9.1 Exclusive Opposition Right

Only partners holding TAILOR BLACK status possess the privilege to formally oppose a tender award.

Valid Grounds for Opposition:

  • Proven capacity misrepresentation by winner
  • Documented quality failures on prior platform work
  • Ethical breaches (fraud, harassment, conflict of interest)
  • Anti-competitive behavior

Invalid Grounds:

  • Disagreement with bid amount
  • Personal rivalry or preference
  • Speculation without evidence

9.2 Review Process

  1. SUBMISSION: Tailor Black partner submits formal opposition with supporting evidence to black@tailors.lk
  2. PRELIMINARY REVIEW: Tailors.lk governance team assesses validity within 3 business days. Invalid oppositions dismissed with explanation.
  3. INVESTIGATION: If valid, winner notified of opposition (details redacted). Evidence reviewed; additional info requested. Timeline: 7-10 business days.
  4. DECISION: Upheld: Tender re-evaluated; may be re-awarded. Dismissed: Original award stands; no appeal.
  5. CONFIDENTIALITY: All opposition details kept confidential. Retaliation against opposing partner prohibited.

Warning: Abuse of this privilege (frivolous oppositions) may result in Tailor Black status review.

Note: Tailor Black status cannot be bought. It is earned through consistent excellence (2+ years as Verified partner, 4.8+ rating, zero complaints, community contribution) and invitation-only review.
